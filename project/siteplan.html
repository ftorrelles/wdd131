Site Name

NexDevp

The name comes from combining "Nex" (representing "Conexión" word in Spanish) and "Devp" (Development). I chose this name because it represents my goal of connecting businesses with the next level of technological development. It is short, memorable, and professional for a digital agency.

Site Purpose

NexDevp is a portfolio and service-based website designed to offer professional web development, management software solutions, digital marketing, and AI automation.

The site serves as a central hub where potential clients can explore my services, view past projects, and contact me to initiate digital transformation for their businesses.

Scenarios

Questions my target audience (business owners, entrepreneurs) might ask:

  • "I have a small business and want to automate my customer replies. How can your AI services help me save time?"
  • "I need a custom website that is faster than my current one. Do you use modern technologies like React or simple WordPress templates?"
